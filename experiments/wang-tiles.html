Wang/WFC is by-example: it recombines patches cut from the source, so the output inherits the source's seam quality — it can't invent shorter lines than the example contains. Merging dissolves patch-boundary seams where crossing tiles let it. Deterministic per (params, seed); tiles infinitely by hashing lattice coords.
